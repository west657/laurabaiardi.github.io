About Me

Laura Baiardi

Laura Baiardi is a professional writer with extensive experience as a copywriter, ghostwriter, and proofreader. From a young age, she felt a natural calling to craft worlds through words.

She has taught Italian to foreign students, working as a language coach and L2 Italian teacher. She attended the Scuola del Fumetto (Comic Book School) in Milan and holds a degree in artistic makeup.

She is deeply interested in Artificial Intelligence and how emerging technologies are transforming the world of writing and the arts. As a film audio describer, she translates images into words for blind and visually impaired audiences.

Audio Description

Translating images into words

Audio description means translating images into words. An audio describer is a film adapter who writes a script designed to be read by an off-screen narrator. Their role consists of describing every scene of a movie — facial expressions, physical traits of characters, settings, and actions — for the benefit of a blind or visually impaired audience.

Publications

La Ballata di Smitty Wesson

New Mexico, 1889. Smitty Wesson is a horse thief on the run, hunted by private vendettas and old grudges. Riding his horse Quicksand, he finds refuge in Silvertown, a gritty town of cattlemen and gambling, where he crosses paths with Mike Jacobs—a schoolteacher determined to find a hired gun to avenge a blood wrong. Amidst schemes, daring escapes, pointed guns, and dangerous alliances, the two men find themselves bound by a shared destiny. But in the wild West, every choice comes with a price, and every friendship can turn into betrayal. Shootouts, forbidden passions, and intersecting revenges bring to life a fast-paced western, where the law is written by the barrel of a gun.

Format · Global Webinar

Global Webinar – Digital Event & Workshop

This international live-streaming format brings the author directly into the heart of the novel, eliminating any logistical hurdles or travel expenses for the organizer. An event accessible from anywhere in the world. The original text is used as an educational tool, engaging expats, students of the Italian language abroad, and cinema and literature enthusiasts in a compelling reading and text analysis experience.

The event culminates in a parallel between the Far West frontier and our digital era, sparking a debate between history and contemporary times. At the end of the experience, students take part in a creative writing workshop based on the characters and settings of the story.

Benefits for the language school or association: No travel costs or transport logistics. Boosts follower engagement, delivers high-quality social media content, and attracts potential new members.
